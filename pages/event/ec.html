Death Race:EC-DEATH

Min Team:4 Members

Max Team: 4 Members

Department: Computer

Price: INR 50 per Head


  • Event Description

    Survival of the strongest. Here the ROBO will face the moving obstacles like, open close gates, Tunnels, See-Saw, Flapper, Water, Bridges etc.“One who falls behind is left behind”. So pack your bags and pump your bots because it’s get, set and GO!!!

  • Events Rules
    1. All the required accessories have to be brought by the participants.
    2. The participants are not allowed to damage the track, or indulge in any argument with the coordinator as this will lead to disqualification of their robot.
    3. Coordinators reserve the right to ask for explanation of the robot.
    4. The wireless remote control sets must be handed over to the event coordinator at the time of reporting, so as to avoid any illegal control of the Robot due to interference caused by the intentional or unintentional matching of radio frequencies of different participants.
    5. In case of wire entanglement, the timers will not be stopped and the race will be restarted from the previous checkpoint.
    6. Sufficient time will be given to the team before the start to study the arena and devise game play strategy.
    7. Certificate of participation will be given to all participants can take actively part in event. If student will register and not attain the event, they cannot get certificate.
    8. In case of wire entanglement, the timers will not be stopped and the race will be restarted from the previous checkpoint.
  • Evaluation Criterias
    Round 1

    Elimination Round.
    Participants will complete the track in given time interval. Who will
    complete the track in minimum time interval; will be selected for next round.


    Round 2 (Day 2)

    Elimination Round.
    Participants will complete the track in given time interval.
    Who will complete the track in minimum time interval, will selected for Runner up or winner

  • Requirements
    Round 1: D2-Building (Day 1)
    Final Round: D2-Buiding (Day 2)

Bowling ManiaEC-BOWLING

Min Team:2 Members

Max Team: 2 Members

Department: Computer

Price: INR 50 per Head


  • Event Description

    The feeling of the ball hitting the pins, hearing and seeing them fall down, is deeply gratifying. We have all felt that instantaneous reward. It’s about control and chaos. Show off your bowling swag. Once you start bowling you won’t stop

  • Events Rules

    1. Coordinators reserve the rights to ask for explanation of your bowling action.
    2. Points will be deducted if the bowl touches any of the live hurdles..
    3. Faculty and coordinator decision will be the final decision.
    4. Damaging of any element will lead to direct disqualification.
    5. Certificate of participant will be given to all participants who take part actively in the event.
    6. If student will register and not attain the event, they won’t get the certificate.

  • Evaluation Criterias
    Round 1

    1. Elimination round. Participant must be able to put down maximum bowling-pins.
    2. 12 chances will be given to every team.
    3. Participant team need to score maximum points by putting down the bottle pins kept at the end of the bowling alley. Points will be deducted if the bowl touches any of the hurdle.


    Round 2 (Day 2)

    1. Final Round.
    2. The team with Maximum number of points will be announced as winner.
    3. If required, the teams will also be judged on the basis of time taken by them to complete their chances.

  • Requirements
    Round 1: D2-Building (Day 1)
    Final Round: D2-Buiding (Day 2)
