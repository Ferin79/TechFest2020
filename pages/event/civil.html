Skyward Receptacle CIVIL-SKY

Min Team:4 Members

Max Team: 4 Members

Department: Civil

Price: INR 50 per Head

  • Events Details

    Objective

    The event is based on designing of water tank, arrangements of structural members, size of members and their structural skills will be tasted.


    Events Rules:

    1. Instructions will be given on the spot.
    2. Dimensions of the structure will be given on the spot.
    3. Students have to construct frame structure of water tank.
    4. The evaluation of the points will be decided by how much water your water tank structure can bear.
    5. The team whose water tank structure holding maximum amount of water will be declared as winning team.
