Blind Game

Event Code: APPLIED-BLIND

Min Team:4 Members

Max Team: 4 Members

Department: Computer

Price: INR 50 per Head


Event Description

This event is combination of knowledge, fun & games. There will be 3 Rounds


Round 1 Quiz (Pan & Paper round)

1. Solve 25-30 questions.
2. Max.Time limit- 30 min
3. Elimination based on marks will be given from answer of questions


Round 2 Fun Games

1. Max.Time limit: 20 min
2. Elimination based on Fastest Fingers First


Round 3 Complete the Path

1. Max.Time limit: 20 min
2. Elimination based on Fastest Fingers First


Evaluation Criteria:

1. Elimination will be based on the completion of the assigned task at the earliest in each round.
2. A Candidate who completes all the levels will be declared as a winner.
3. There will be no negative marking.
4. The winner of round 2& 3 will be based on lowest time taken for the complete the task. 3/8
5. If participant make any mistake or foul the 10 seconds add to their time will be there panty.


Events Rules:

1. There will be 3 rounds(ROUND 1,2& FINAL ROUND)
2. Each team have consist of 4 participants. (Not more then 4)
3. Questions shall be in the form of multiple choice, True/False statement, Specific-answer, One word-answer, Error solving, output etc.
4. The time limit will be strictly monitored according to event start time. Any violation of the same will lead to immediate disqualification without notice.
5. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers will be final.
6. No discussion between participants is allowed once the event starts, otherwise, the organizers have the full authority to disqualify the participants.
7. Participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
8. All participants will be given participations certificate.
9. The organizing team has the right to change the rules and regulations of event.

Box Cricket

Event Code: APPLIED-CRIC

Min Team:4 Members

Max Team: 4 Members

Department: D-4

Price: INR 50 per Head


Event Description

Box cricket or indoor cricket is a variant of cricket that shares many basic concepts with the original game but with added enhancements to improve the engagement and enjoyment of casual players. Different variants exist within box cricket based on the abilities and availability of players for the match.


Events Rules:

1.) Four playermust per team.
2.) Each team have 4-6 overs.
3.) There is one powerover which can use by team any time, in power over run may double. During power over: 1 run = 2 run Wide & no-ball = 5 run 1 wicket = (-3) run
4.) over is equally divided for each player.
4.) Underarm bowling is compulsory.
5.) your team must have to reach the ground before 15 minute of allocated time else the team will be disqualified.
6.) after being disqualified the registration fee will not be refunded.
7.) extra run will given similar as cricket rules ( i.e no-ball, wide ball, legby, over throw ).
