E-treasure Hunt: COMP-ETRAS

Min Team:1 Members

Max Team: 1 Members

Department: Computer

Price: INR 50 per Head


  • Event Description

    “If you gaze deep enough, you can reach the treasure.” It is a mind stretching event. There will be 3 levels.


    Level 1: Solving Logical Reasoning (Pen & Paper Round)

    Rules:
    1. Solve 20-25 Questions.
    2. Maximum time limit - 30 minutes.
    3. Elimination based on - Fastest Fingers First


    Level 2: Solving Riddles (Pen & Paper Round)

    Rules:
    1. Solve 2-3 Riddles to get Visualization Puzzle.
    2. Maximum time limit - 15 minutes.
    3. Elimination based on - Fastest Fingers First


    Level 3: Visualization Puzzle (Googling)

    Rules:
    1. Trace the Clue from the Artwork.
    2. Maximum time limit - 20 minutes.
    3. Elimination based on - Fastest Fingers First

  • Events Rules

    1. The time limit will be strictly monitored according to event start time. Any violation of the same will lead to immediate disqualification without notice.
    2. The answer can range from a word to a phrase or may completely and utterly surprise you.
    3. No clarifications or doubts about the questions will be entertained.
    4. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers will be final.
    5. No discussion between the participants is allowed once the event starts; otherwise, the organizers have the full authority to disqualify the participants.
    6. All participants will be given participation certificates.
    7. The organizing team has the right to change the rules and regulations of the event.

  • Evaluation Criterias

    1. Elimination is based on the completion of the assigned task at the earliest in each round.
    2. A candidate who completes all levels will be declared as a winner.

  • Requirements

    Round 1: 4 Classrooms (45 mins)
    Round 2: 1 Classroom (30 mins)
    Round 3: D1-203 (30 mins)

Relay Coding: COMP-RELAY

Min Team:2 Members

Max Team: 2 Members

Department: Computer

Price: INR 50 per Head


  • Event Description

    Relay Coding is the programming event which mainly focuses on the programming and ability to understand the Coding between 2 participants.

    Round 1

    1. Statement will be given at the time of event.
    2. Platform: Ubuntu.
    3. Tools: Code blocks, Gedit
    4. Compiler: gcc
    5. Language: ‘C/C++’
    6. Time Limit: 30 minutes.
    7. In 30 minutes,
    a. First 10 minutes will be given to First participant
    b. Next 10 minutes will be given to second participant to complete the remaining code
    c. Remaining 10 minutes, both can code together.


    Round 2

    1. Statement will be given at the time of event.
    2. Platform: Ubuntu.
    3. Tools: VS Code,Code blocks, Gedit
    4. Compiler: gcc
    5. Language: ‘C/C++’
    6. Time Limit: 60 minutes.
    7. In 60 minutes,
    a. First 30 minutes will be given to First participant
    b. Next 30 minutes will be given to second participant to complete the remaining code

  • Events Rules

    1. Participation: Exact 2 participants
    i. Event Time Duration: i. 30 minutes (Preliminary Round) ii. 60 minutes (Final Round)
    2. The prerequisite for participation in this event is sound knowledge of the C/C++Programming.
    3. The participants should report at the venue 15 minutes before the scheduled time of the event.
    4. Internet usage will not be allowed to the participants.
    5. If a lesser number of teams register for this event, there will be only final round.
    6. If any rules are found to be broken by any of the team members, it will lead to their disqualification.
    7. The decision of the event organizers would be final and shall not be subject to any change

  • Evaluation Criterias

    1. Coordination
    2. Problem Completion Level
    3. Logic

  • Requirements: Lab/Class

    Round 1: D1-107(1)(30 mins)
    Round 2: D1-107(1)(60 mins)
